Annotation Type UsingScope


  • @Target(value=TYPE)
@Retention(value=RUNTIME)
@Inherited
@Documented
public @interface UsingScope
    An annotation on an InjectedEntryPoint that binds a scope annotation to an implementation. Provisions and Bindings can also be annotated with this to indicate additional scopes defined in the entire transitive closure of binding definitions.

    This overrides the scope annotation's own ScopeImplementor.
